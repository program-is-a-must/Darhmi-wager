About WagePro

Your smart companion for wage tracking and financial clarity

What is WagePro?

Built for workers who deserve financial clarity

WagePro is a modern web application designed to help individuals calculate, track, and manage their earnings with ease. Whether you're a freelancer, employee, or business owner, WagePro gives you the tools to understand your income better.

  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Fast, accurate calculations with tax support
  • Overtime and bonus handling built-in
  • Persistent history stored in your browser
  • No sign-up or download required
💼
Our Mission

Simplifying income tracking for everyone

We believe everyone — from market traders to corporate staff — should have access to clear, powerful tools for managing their earnings. WagePro is free, fast, and built for real people.

💰 Smart Calculations

Instantly calculate wages with overtime, tax deductions, and bonuses. Get a complete earnings breakdown in seconds.

📊 Detailed Insights

Understand your income at a daily, weekly, and monthly level with clean, easy-to-read results.

💾 Persistent History

Keep a running log of all your wage calculations. Data is saved locally — no account needed, no data shared.

📱 Universal Access

WagePro works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop browsers without any installation.
